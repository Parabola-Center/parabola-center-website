  We know more about cannabis than perhaps ever before, but somehow the research and the people who need it most keep missing each other. Researchers often publish papers that never leave the academic world, so policymakers and advocates miss out on key data and findings.


  Join Parabola Center for a webinar to bring together voices from across these communities. We'll demystify the cannabis research process and build a bridge between the experts who produce research and the regulators, activists, and journalists who should be using it.


  Panelists will explore how academic research is planned and designed, including how you can be part of that process, and how it can contribute to real-world policy. Attendees will leave with concrete, actionable steps to more effectively forge partnerships between researchers and people working on the ground.


  **What to expect to take away:**


  - Organizers will learn how to engage with the research process and find academic partners

  - Regulators will learn practical ways to connect with academic researchers and incorporate their findings

  - Researchers will learn how to translate and amplify their work beyond the academic world
